Welcome to Kakaw Galleon

Artisanal Chocolates Crafted with Passion

Our Story

Kakaw Galleon stands as a specialty chocolate business dedicated to crafting exceptional artisanal chocolates using locally sourced, premium ingredients. Our foundation stems from a deep appreciation for the rich cocoa history and culture of the Philippines, driving our commitment to sustainability and fair trade.

Our Mission

Kakaw Galleon's mission extends beyond the creation of delectable treats. We invite you to experience our products, each a testament to our dedication to a more sustainable and equitable world. Whether you're a chocolate enthusiast seeking exquisite flavors or a conscious shopper eager to make a difference, our chocolates represent an opportunity to savor exceptional quality while contributing to meaningful change through your support.
